Our Mission

Transfer Credit Match is designed to streamline the process of transferring academic credits. By mapping Knowledge Units (KUs) to courses and implementing smoother transitions between institutions.

Efficient Credit Transfers

Our system analyzes the content of courses to determine if they meet the requirements for a direct transfer of credits.

Secure & Reliable

Built with security in mind, our backend ensures that user data and academic records are protected through role-based access and robust authentication mechanisms.

User-Friendly Interface

We have developed user-friendly APIs and a simple frontend that allow administrators and students to interact with the system easily.
